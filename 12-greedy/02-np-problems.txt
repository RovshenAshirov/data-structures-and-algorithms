# NP-muammolar. Traveling salesperson

NP-Complete Problems
Traveling Salesperson Problem

Musofir savdogar muammosi:
    - Savdogar ishi bilan safarga chiqdi va safar davomida 5ta manzilga kirib o'tishi kerak.
    - Muammo: 5ta manzilga eng kam vaqt (yoki masofa) bosib kirib o'tish yo'lini topish 

three-address.png

4ta bo'lsachi? A, B, C, D
4ta manzil uchun kombinatsiyalar soni 4! = 1 * 2 * 3 * 4 = 24
Demak nta nuqta uchun kombinatsiyalar soni n! ga teng:
    5! = 120
    6! = 720
    10! = 3 628 800

Big O: O(n!)
